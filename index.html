Welcome to ________

This site serves the purpose of approaching various subjects that are connected with the big questions in life: Where do we come from? What does it mean to be human? Is there an ultimate purpose? What can we know regarding death? During my time at university, a certain reality was continually stressed: no human has perfectly escaped ther own natural bias, presuppositions, and varying degrees of ignorance. In that spirit, I must admit that I am approaching this site from the perspective of a believer in the Lord Jesus Christ. It is my hope that this fact will not prevent you from using this site, but will be seen as an opportunity to better understand one who may hold a position other your own. One of my desires for this site is that everyone perusing its pages may find material for quality reflection, the furtherance of thought and subtance for healthy and respectful dialogue.

This site has been developed to thrive on links. The core reason for this is that these subjects are all interconnected. While each subject is distinct, and should be approached as such for the sake of gaining greater bredth and depth of knowledge in that subject, ultimately all the topics on this subject can be traced back to foundational points. On the path to these foundational points, these subjects will intertwine. Additionally, as you revisit this site and continually read the material, you will have questions arise as a natural result of your studying this material. The many links have been put in place to help stream line the process of your personal inquiry.
